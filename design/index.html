Common questions

Before you call,
the basics.

Still wondering something? Pick up the phone — we answer on weekdays between 8 and 6.
Are estimates really free?

Yes. We visit your site, measure, listen and leave a written proposal within 5 business days — no obligation, no deposit.

What areas do you serve?

Northern Virginia — Fairfax, Ashburn, Leesburg, Gainesville, Vienna, McLean, Arlington and Manassas. Outside the ring? Call us; we take on larger projects further out.

How long does a typical project take?

A standard paver patio: 5–10 working days. A full outdoor living room with fireplace: 4–6 weeks. We give written timelines before we start.

Do you pull permits?

Yes. As a Class A contractor we handle all required county permits, HOA approvals and Miss Utility locates.

What’s your warranty?

Two years on our workmanship, plus whatever the material manufacturer offers (typically 25 years on pavers). If something settles or shifts, we come back.

Do you speak Spanish?

Sí. Toda nuestra comunicación — cotizaciones, contratos, actualizaciones diarias — está disponible en inglés o español.

About Lomar GCG

Family-owned.
Twenty years on Virginia job-sites.

We build so you can live outside.

Lomar Scapes Solutions started as two brothers laying a patio on a weekend in Manassas. Two decades later, we're a full-service general contractor building outdoor spaces across the Northern Virginia metro.
